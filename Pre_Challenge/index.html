A message

Most people in Germany associate negative thoughts with Iran. This is mostly because the daily news are only talking about their government. Recently, I made the long journey to this country which is the home country of many of my friends. I visited friends of friends and I made new friends on the street. Everyone was really friendly. Many Iranians don't support their own government. Especially young and educated Iranians are very open minded but they still preserver there culture well. You can find many well maintained monuments in Iran such as Persepolis, the city of Persians, which was buiilt 2500 years ago. You can safely travel on your own in this country.
